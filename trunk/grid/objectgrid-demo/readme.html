Lab 4: Distributed Grid introduction

  1. In fr.xebia.demo.objectgrid.AbstractObjectGridTest, switch useLocalGrid = true; to useLocalGrid = false;
  2. Launch fr.xebia.demo.objectgrid.launcher.ObjectGridCatalogServerLauncher.main() to start a catalog service on your workstation
  3. Launch fr.xebia.demo.objectgrid.launcher.ObjectGridContainer1Launcher.main() and fr.xebia.demo.objectgrid.launcher.ObjectGridContainer2Launcher.main() to start two containers on your workstation
  4. A fully functional Object grid with one Catalog Service and two containers is running on your workstation
  5. Play with previous test cases (EmployeeDaoObjectGridImplTest and EmployeeSalaryProcessorAgentTest)

Lab 4: Distributed Xebia Grid !

  1. Stop ObjectGridContainer1Launcher.main() and ObjectGridContainer2Launcher.main(), replace localhost bye the actual ip in the launchers and then start them
  2. Your ObjectGrid containers are connected to the Xebia Grid !
  3. In fr.xebia.demo.objectgrid.AbstractObjectGridTest, switch catalogServerAddresses = "localhost:2809" to catalogServerAddresses = "${the-ip-of-the-shared-catalog-service}:2809"
  4. Play with previous test cases (EmployeeDaoObjectGridImplTest and EmployeeSalaryProcessorAgentTest)
